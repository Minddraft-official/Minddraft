Three steps to a better session

No setup. No learning curve. Just open it and start.

1

Check in with your mood

Pick one of five emotions. MindDraft uses this to recommend the right study technique, ambient sound, and whether you should do a quick breathing reset first.

2

Let MindDraft set the scene

Your music starts, the timer is configured for your energy level, and you get a gentle, non-overwhelming plan for the next hour.

3

Study, log, and build your streak

After each session, your mood and focus are logged. Over time, MindDraft shows you your patterns — peak hours, stress cycles, and what actually works for you.
